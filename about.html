I'm Jephte Dufresne

Hi, I’m Jephte Dufresne! A Senior Creative Designer and Artificial Intelligence Automation Expert. I've been designing for almost 5 years now, finding my love for creating clean and modern designs to turn clients ideas into visuals that are easy to understand and enjoyable to use. Whether it’s logos, websites, videos, or apps, I focus on making final deliveries look great through aestheticism or modernism. I’m a strong AI generalist, having dived straight into the industry a few years ago, I have found ways to automate many processes for my previous employer, including onboarding other employees and managing 4 different teams. As the youngest project manager in my branch, I’ve organized events and sent mass content and emails to clients with the help of my knowledge in the AI field. As a side quest, I'm a content creator on YouTube with a community of over 10K active viewers and active socials on TikTok, Instagram, Discord, and Twitch. Having worked for 3 different content creator channels summing up to 100K subs, I have grounded experience in the social media industry.Born in the Caribbean, and based in Nairobi, Kenya, I’m always exploring new ways to combine design, storytelling, and a little personality to bring projects to life.
